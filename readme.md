<h1 align="center">Physiological Attributes of Elite Marathon Athletes</h1>
<img src="./images/runner.jpg" align="right" width="220" alt="Kelvin Kiptum during his world record run at the 2023 Chicago marathon">
Kelvin Kiptum during his world record run at the 2023 Chicago marathon. Elite marathon runners such as him typically have a V&#775;O<sub>2</sub> maximum exceeding 80 ml/kg/minute. For reference, healthy, untrained men typically have a V&#775;O<sub>2</sub> maximum in the range of 35-40 ml/kg/minute.
